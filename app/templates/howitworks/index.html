How it Works

Follow the process of posting to LionsMatter. Start to finish.

Step 1: You post to LionsMatter

Got a question, comment, or suggestion for any department at TCNJ? Here's your chance to tell us! Posts on LionsMatter are visible to everyone on campus.

Details:

1. Pick your name.
You can post to LionsMatter using your full name, TCNJ username, or "anonymous" by setting the "post as" () field.

2. Choose media.
You can add a relevant photo or Vine to your post and preview the image/Vine before submitting it.

3. Submit to a department.
You can select which department to submit your post to from the department dropdown option.

Step 2: The College Weighs In

Next, students at the college vote and comment on your post. Ultimately, the more attention a post receives the more visible it will become. In this way LionsMatter can reveal the most important issues on campus.

Step 3: Student Government Sorts Your Post

A group of dedicated Student Government members sort through the posts on LionsMatter making sure each is sent to the right place.
